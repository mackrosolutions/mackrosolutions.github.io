Local to Toronto since 2012, Mackro Solutions was founded by an aspiring entrepreneur after her tenure at Perry Krieger & Associates Inc. and BDO Canada. Devoted to personal insolvency, this small team of investigators track down creditors who are owed money from delinquent estates held in trust by the governing body of trustees in bankruptcy.
If you have been contacted by us, you will have all the information you need. If you haven't been contacted by us - you are not owed any money ( yet :) ).
